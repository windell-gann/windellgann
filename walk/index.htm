    Explanation of the Program

    A few years ago at the Rogersville Church of Christ I planned a year's program to preach through the Bible. The plan involved teaching the auditorium Bible class with an overview of the Bible book and then preaching from the book in the worship service. A hand-out was prepared to go along with the Bible class and on the back page was the sermon outline.

    These lessons are presented here for whatever use you may make of them to the glory of the Lord. I don't claim originality, much of the material was gathered from many various sources. Therefore you should not publish this for sell, but if you can use it in your Bible teaching program God bless you.

    The material is available in two formats. The format for viewing can also be copied and used with your word processor and be reformatted as you wish. I use WordPerfect and these files are made available for downloading which contain several graphic files and formatted to print out on a 5.5 x 8.5 page. They are intended to printed out using Word Perfect's "booklet" feature. Generally the first three pages contain the Bible class material, and page four the sermon outline.
